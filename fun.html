CLUB/BAR/PARTY (21+)

While we don’t have a very strong Greek life, there are definitely parties happening for all students. Always be on the lookout for drunk and sketchy people at parties, but you should be fine if you are in a group of friends. After all, everyone is trying to have a good time!

While we don’t condone underage drinking, if you decide to break the law, try at least to understand your alcohol tolerance and your limits; and don’t drink pre-mixed stuff that you don’t fully trust.

Be mindful of how much money you spend on weekends; especially when using a credit card.

There are many different atmospheres when it comes to bars in Chicago: make sure you try a little bit of everything, so not to miss out! From the sports and college bars of Wrigleyville, to the fancy lounges of River North, to the craft breweries of Logan Square, the city has so much to offer!
